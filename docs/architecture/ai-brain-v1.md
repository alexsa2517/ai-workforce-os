# AI Brain Architecture v1

Version: 1.0

Status: Planning

---

# Vision

The AI Brain is the central intelligence of AI Workforce OS.

Every AI Employee shares the same Brain.

Only their skills are different.

---

# AI Brain Modules

## 1. Input Processor

Receive user messages.

Responsibilities

- Detect language
- Clean message
- Understand intent

---

## 2. Memory

Remember

- Customer
- Conversation
- Preferences
- Purchase history

---

## 3. Knowledge

Business knowledge

- Products
- FAQ
- Policies
- Promotions

---

## 4. Reasoning

Think before answering.

Responsibilities

- Analyze context
- Compare information
- Generate best response

---

## 5. Decision

Choose what to do.

Example

- Answer directly
- Recommend products
- Ask more questions
- Transfer to human

---

## 6. Communication

Generate natural responses.

Goals

- Friendly
- Professional
- Human-like

---

## 7. Learning

Improve over time.

Learn from

- Human corrections
- Customer feedback
- Sales results

---

## 8. Safety

Prevent

- Wrong information
- Unsafe responses
- Privacy leaks
