# Sales AI Employee #001

Version: 1.0

Status: Planning

Owner: AI Workforce OS

---

# Overview

Sales AI Employee #001 is the first AI Employee developed under AI Workforce OS.

Its mission is to help businesses automatically respond to customers on Facebook Messenger, answer product questions, recommend products, and assist in closing sales 24/7.

---

# Vision

Create an AI Sales Employee that works like a real sales representative.

---

# Goals

- Reply to customers within seconds.
- Answer product questions accurately.
- Recommend products.
- Remember customer conversations.
- Assist in closing sales.
- Transfer conversations to a human when necessary.

---

# Target Users

- Small Business
- Online Sellers
- Facebook Fanpage Owners
- E-commerce Businesses

---

# MVP Features

## Customer Chat

- Welcome customer
- Answer FAQs
- Answer product information
- Recommend products

## Product Knowledge

- Product name
- Description
- Price
- Stock
- Promotion

## Customer Memory

Remember:

- Customer Name
- Previous Conversations
- Interested Products
- Purchase Status

## Human Handoff

Transfer the conversation when:

- AI is not confident.
- Customer requests a human.
- Customer complaint.

## Dashboard

Business Owner can view:

- Total Conversations
- Leads
- Orders
- Revenue
- AI Performance

---

# Success Criteria

The MVP is successful when:

- AI can answer customer questions.
- AI can recommend products.
- AI can summarize customer orders.
- 10 stores test the system.
- 5 stores become paying customers.
